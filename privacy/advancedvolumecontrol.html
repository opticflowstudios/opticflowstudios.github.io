Privacy Policy for Advanced Volume Control Extension

This Privacy Policy describes how the "Advanced Volume Control" Chrome Extension (the "Extension") handles your information.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

The Advanced Volume Control Extension is designed to operate entirely within your browser. It does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, browsing history, audio preferences, or any other user-specific information. All functionalities, including master volume control and individual tab volume adjustments, are processed locally on your device.

2. Permissions Used

The Extension requires the following permission to function:

  • "tabs": This permission allows the Extension to:
    • Identify currently open tabs that are playing audio.
    • Read the title and favicon of these tabs to display them in the popup.
    • Adjust the volume and mute state of individual tabs.
    • The Extension only interacts with tab audio properties and does not access or read the content of your web pages or any sensitive data.

3. Data Storage

No user data is stored persistently by this Extension, either locally or remotely. All volume settings applied are transient and only affect the current browsing session or until the browser is closed or the tab is reloaded, unless Chrome's native behavior retains them.

4. Third-Party Services

The Advanced Volume Control Extension does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics providers, or advertising networks.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the Extension's listing (e.g., on the Chrome Web Store) or by updating the policy text accessible through the Extension, if applicable. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the contact information provided on the Chrome Web Store page for this Extension.
