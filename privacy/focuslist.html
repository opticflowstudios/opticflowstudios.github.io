Privacy Policy for FocusList Chrome Extension

This Privacy Policy describes how the FocusList Chrome Extension ("the Extension") collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Data Collection

The FocusList Extension is designed with user privacy as a priority. It does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information from your browsing activity or your device on external servers.

All data, specifically your to-do list tasks, is stored locally within your Chrome browser's chrome.storage.sync API. This means your tasks are only accessible by your Chrome browser and are synchronized across your logged-in Chrome profiles if you have sync enabled.

2. Use of Data

The tasks you enter into FocusList are used solely for the purpose of displaying and managing your to-do list within the Extension itself. They are not used for tracking, advertising, or any other purpose.

3. Data Sharing

No data collected or stored by the FocusList Extension is shared with any third parties.

4. Data Security

Your tasks, stored locally via chrome.storage.sync, are secured by Chrome's built-in security features. While the Extension takes measures to ensure data integrity within the local storage, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the Extension's description or through an update notification in the Chrome Web Store. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer through the Chrome Web Store page for FocusList.
